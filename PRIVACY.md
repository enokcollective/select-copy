# Privacy Policy for Select Copy

_Last updated: 2026-06-01_

Select Copy does **not** collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal
information or user content. Everything happens locally in your browser.

## What the extension does with your data

- **Selected text** is copied to your own system clipboard, locally, on your
  device. It is never sent to us or to any third party, and it is not logged or
  retained by the extension.
- **Your settings** (whether Select Copy is on or off, and whether the on-page
  "copied" banner is shown) are stored locally on your device using
  `chrome.storage.local`. They never leave your browser.

## What the extension does NOT do

- No analytics or telemetry.
- No tracking or advertising.
- No remote servers; the extension makes no network requests.
- No third-party services or data sharing.

## Permissions

- **`storage`**: remembers your on/off and banner preferences on your device.
- **Host access (`http://*/*`, `https://*/*`)**: required so the extension can
  detect when you select text on the pages you visit and copy it to your
  clipboard. The extension only reads your current text selection for the purpose
  of copying it; it does not otherwise read, collect, or transmit page content.
